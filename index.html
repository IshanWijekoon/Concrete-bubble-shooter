How To Play

Lock the grid, match the bubbles, and keep the vault stable.

  • Move your pointer to aim the launcher.
  • Click or tap to fire the current bubble.
  • Match 3 or more bubbles of the same color to clear them.
  • Disconnected bubbles fall away for bonus score.
  • Every few turns, pressure rows descend. Survive the stack.
